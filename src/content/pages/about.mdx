This is an open source school magazine website build with everything new in [Next.js](https://nextjs.org/).

We are not just another school magazine club, we are a hub of creativity and intellectual exploration.
Our club is dedicated to curating captivating magazines, crafting thought-provoking articles spanning various subjects,
and nurturing creativity within our community. Through our collective efforts, we aim to inspire
and empower the next generation of thinkers and creators. Join us on this exciting journey of knowledge and innovation!

## Website features

- Read the latest [releases](/releases) and [articles](/articles).
- Learn more about our featured authors.
- [Join us](/join-us) as a member to contribute your own articles.
